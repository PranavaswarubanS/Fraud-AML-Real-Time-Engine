# Real-Time Decision Engine for Fraud & AML (Fork)

Open-source **transaction / event / user** decision engine for
**fraud, AML, and abuse** detection. Includes no-code rules, real-time & batch
execution, and case management, with auditability by design.


---

## 🌐 Open Architecture

- **Composable** — Connect internal systems (transaction DBs, KYC providers, 3rd-party data).
- **Self-host or licensed** — Open-source core; enterprise add-ons available.
- **Privacy-first** — Self-host to keep data within your infra.

> We do not provide KYC services. You can integrate your preferred vendors.
